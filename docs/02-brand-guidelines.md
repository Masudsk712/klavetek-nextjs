# 1. Brand Overview

KLAVETEK Green Blocks & Tiles Pvt. Ltd. is a modern building materials manufacturer focused on delivering high-quality AAC Blocks and construction solutions.

The digital experience should communicate innovation, engineering precision, sustainability, and long-term trust.

---

# 2. Brand Vision

To become one of India's most trusted and technologically advanced AAC Blocks manufacturers.

---

# 3. Brand Mission

Provide builders, contractors, architects, and developers with premium-quality construction materials backed by engineering excellence and sustainable manufacturing.

---

# 4. Brand Values

Every digital interaction should reinforce these values:

* Trust
* Quality
* Innovation
* Precision
* Sustainability
* Reliability
* Transparency
* Customer Focus

---

# 5. Brand Personality

The brand should feel:

* Professional
* Confident
* Modern
* Premium
* Engineering-driven
* Minimal
* Honest
* Dependable

Avoid playful or overly casual communication.

---

# 6. Brand Voice

Tone of voice:

* Clear
* Technical where necessary
* Friendly but professional
* Informative
* Confident
* Solution-oriented

Do not use exaggerated marketing claims or unnecessary buzzwords.

---

# 7. Visual Identity

Overall style:

* Industrial Modern
* Minimal Luxury
* High Contrast
* Spacious Layout
* Premium Photography
* Clean Interfaces

Visual priorities:

1. Readability
2. Simplicity
3. Consistency
4. Trust

---

# 8. Logo Guidelines

## Approved Formats

* SVG (preferred)
* PNG (transparent)
* PDF (print)

## Clear Space

Maintain a minimum clear space equal to the height of the "K" around the logo.

## Minimum Size

Desktop:

120px width minimum

Mobile:

80px width minimum

## Incorrect Usage

Do NOT:

* Stretch the logo
* Rotate the logo
* Add shadows
* Change colors
* Add outlines
* Place on low-contrast backgrounds

---

# 9. Color System

## Primary Colors

Graphite Black

Purpose:

* Headlines
* Navigation
* Footer
* Primary text

Concrete Grey

Purpose:

* Backgrounds
* Cards
* Dividers

Pure White

Purpose:

* Main surfaces
* Content areas

---

## Accent Colors

Construction Orange

Purpose:

* Primary CTA
* Important highlights
* Active elements

Eco Green

Purpose:

* Sustainability
* Environmental messaging
* Certifications

---

## Neutral Colors

Light Grey

Border Grey

Muted Text Grey

These colors should support layout and readability without drawing attention.

---

## Primary Font

Geist

Fallback:

Inter

---

## Heading Scale

H1

Section heading

Sub-section heading

Readable

---

# 11. Buttons

Primary

Construction Orange background

White text

Rounded pill shape

Secondary

White background

Dark border

Dark text

Text Button

Underline on hover

No unnecessary decorative effects.

---

# 12. Iconography

Preferred Library

Lucide Icons

Rules:

* Outline style
* Consistent stroke width
* Simple geometry
* No colorful icons

Avoid mixing multiple icon styles.

---

# 13. Photography Style

Use:

* Real factory images
* Manufacturing process
* Engineers at work
* Construction sites
* Product close-ups
* Aerial/drone photography

Avoid:

* Low-resolution images
* Watermarked photos
* Artificial-looking stock imagery

---

# 14. Video Style

Hero videos should include:

* Factory overview
* Machinery in operation
* Product movement
* Drone footage
* Natural lighting

Technical requirements:

* 4K source
* Optimized for web
* Muted
* Loop seamlessly

---

# 15. Illustrations

Illustrations should only be used when photography is not suitable.

Style:

* Minimal
* Technical
* Flat
* Clean

Avoid cartoon or playful illustrations.

---

# 16. UI Tone

The interface should communicate:

* Confidence
* Precision
* Quality
* Simplicity

Whitespace is a design element.

Avoid clutter.

---

# 17. Motion Principles

Motion should:

* Guide attention
* Improve understanding
* Feel natural
* Be subtle

Preferred animations:

* Fade
* Reveal
* Scale
* Parallax
* Counter
* Smooth transitions

Avoid:

* Bounce
* Flash
* Shake
* Infinite spinning
* Distracting effects

---

# 18. Content Tone

Content should be:

* Informative
* Professional
* Easy to understand
* Fact-based
* Customer-focused

Avoid:

* Excessive marketing language
* Unsupported claims
* Long paragraphs
* Technical jargon without explanation

---

# 19. Accessibility

All visual elements must meet accessibility requirements.

Guidelines:

* High color contrast
* Readable typography
* Visible focus states
* Accessible buttons
* Descriptive alt text
* Keyboard-friendly navigation

---

# 20. Consistency Rules

Every page must maintain:

* Same typography
* Same spacing system
* Same button styles
* Same color palette
* Same animation behavior
* Same card design
* Same icon style

Consistency is mandatory across the project.

---

# 21. Brand Do's

* Use premium photography
* Maintain generous whitespace
* Keep layouts clean
* Highlight engineering quality
* Prioritize readability
* Use accent colors sparingly
* Ensure visual consistency

---

# 22. Brand Don'ts

Do NOT:

* Use bright random colors
* Mix multiple font families
* Overuse animations
* Add unnecessary gradients
* Use generic stock construction photos
* Create cluttered layouts
* Break the established design system

---

# 23. Quality Checklist

Before publishing any page, verify:

* Logo used correctly
* Brand colors respected
* Typography consistent
* Images high quality
* Buttons consistent
* Icons consistent
* Motion subtle
* Content aligned with brand voice
* Accessibility reviewed

---

# 24. Future Expansion

The brand system should be flexible enough to support:

* Dealer Portal
* Careers
* Blog
* Product Catalogue
* Resource Center
* Mobile App
* Additional product lines

Without requiring a complete redesign.
