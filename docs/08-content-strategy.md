# 1. Purpose

This document defines the content strategy for the KLAVETEK website.

The goal is to ensure that every page:

* Builds trust
* Educates visitors
* Demonstrates expertise
* Generates enquiries
* Supports SEO
* Reflects the KLAVETEK brand

Content should solve customer questions before they need to ask them.

---

# 2. Content Objectives

Primary objectives:

* Explain AAC Blocks clearly
* Highlight engineering advantages
* Showcase manufacturing quality
* Build brand credibility
* Encourage enquiries
* Support dealer growth
* Improve organic search visibility

# 4. Brand Voice

The KLAVETEK voice should be:

* Professional
* Knowledgeable
* Honest
* Confident
* Helpful
* Technical where appropriate

Avoid exaggerated marketing language.

---

# 5. Tone of Voice

The writing should feel:

* Clear
* Direct
* Informative
* Human
* Solution-oriented

Every sentence should add value.

---

# 6. Content Principles

All content should be:

* Accurate
* Original
* Easy to scan
* Well structured
* Actionable
* Customer-focused

Avoid filler content.

---

# 7. Homepage Strategy

The homepage should answer these questions in order:

1. Who is KLAVETEK?
2. What products do we offer?
3. Why choose AAC Blocks?
4. Why choose KLAVETEK?
5. What makes us different?
6. Can we be trusted?
7. How do I contact the company?

Each section should naturally lead to the next.

# 15. CTA Strategy

Primary CTA

* Get a Quote

Secondary CTAs

* Download Brochure
* Contact Sales
* View Products
* Find a Dealer
* Learn More

CTA placement:

* Hero
* Product Pages
* End of long-form sections
* Footer

Avoid overwhelming users with too many buttons.

---

# 16. Headline Guidelines

Headlines should be:

* Short
* Clear
* Benefit-driven

Examples:

* Build Smarter with AAC Blocks
* Precision Manufacturing. Trusted Performance.
* Engineered for Modern Construction

Avoid vague marketing headlines.

---

# 17. Paragraph Guidelines

Paragraphs should:

* Be concise
* Focus on one idea
* Use plain language
* Break up long text with headings and lists

---

# 18. Technical Content

Technical content should include:

* Clear explanations
* Tables where appropriate
* Diagrams (future)
* Downloadable specifications

Explain technical terms where necessary.

---

# 19. FAQ Standards

Each FAQ should:

* Address real customer questions
* Be concise
* Provide direct answers
* Link to related resources when appropriate

Avoid duplicate questions.

---

# 20. Testimonials

Each testimonial should include:

* Customer Name
* Company
* Designation (if available)
* Quote
* Project (optional)

Use authentic testimonials only.

---

# 21. Image Captions

Where captions are used:

* Keep them descriptive
* Add context
* Support the surrounding content

Do not repeat the heading.

---

# 22. Download Content

Every downloadable document should include:

* Title
* Description
* File size
* Version
* Last updated date

Users should know what they are downloading.

---

# 23. Future Content Roadmap

Future additions:

* Blog
* Case Studies
* Industry News
* Technical Articles
* Sustainability Reports
* Careers
* Media Gallery

Content expansion should reinforce KLAVETEK's authority in the industry.

---

# 24. Editorial Guidelines

Before publishing:

* Grammar checked
* Spelling checked
* Fact verified
* Brand voice maintained
* SEO reviewed
* Readability reviewed

Content should be reviewed before publication.

---

# 25. Content Governance

Content ownership:

* Marketing team prepares content
* Technical team verifies specifications
* Project owner approves
* Website team publishes

Maintain version history for major content updates.

---

# 26. Content QA Checklist

Before any page goes live:

* Clear headline
* Structured layout
* Accurate information
* Strong CTA
* SEO metadata completed
* Internal links added
* Images optimized
* Alt text written
* Grammar reviewed
* Mobile readability verified
